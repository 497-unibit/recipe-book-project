Title:
Banana Pancakes

Description:
Fluffy homemade pancakes with ripe bananas, perfect for a quick breakfast.

Ingredients (comma-separated):
2 ripe bananas, 2 eggs, 150g flour, 1 tsp baking powder, 100ml milk, 1 tbsp sugar, butter for frying

Instructions (one step per line):
1. Mash the bananas in a bowl until smooth.
2. Beat in the eggs and milk.
3. Mix in flour, baking powder, and sugar until just combined.
4. Heat butter in a pan over medium heat.
5. Pour small portions of batter and cook until bubbles form on top.
6. Flip and cook the other side until golden brown.
7. Serve with honey or maple syrup.

Title:
Caprese Salad

Description:
A simple Italian salad with fresh mozzarella, tomatoes, and basil drizzled with olive oil.

Ingredients (comma-separated):
3 large tomatoes, 250g fresh mozzarella, fresh basil leaves, 3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil, 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar, salt, black pepper

Instructions (one step per line):
1. Slice tomatoes and mozzarella into even rounds.
2. Arrange them alternately on a plate.
3. Tuck fresh basil leaves between the slices.
4. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar.
5. Season with salt and pepper.
6. Serve immediately.

Title:
Beef Tacos

Description:
Seasoned ground beef tacos loaded with fresh toppings in crispy shells.

Ingredients (comma-separated):
500g ground beef, 1 onion, 2 cloves garlic, 1 tbsp cumin, 1 tsp paprika, 8 taco shells, lettuce, 2 tomatoes, 100g cheddar cheese, sour cream, salt

Instructions (one step per line):
1. Dice the onion and mince the garlic.
2. Brown the ground beef in a large pan over medium-high heat.
3. Add onion and garlic and cook until softened.
4. Stir in cumin, paprika, and salt. Cook for 2 more minutes.
5. Warm the taco shells according to package instructions.
6. Shred the lettuce, dice the tomatoes, and grate the cheese.
7. Fill each shell with beef and top with lettuce, tomato, cheese, and sour cream.

Title:
Mushroom Risotto

Description:
A creamy Italian rice dish slowly cooked with mushrooms and parmesan.

Ingredients (comma-separated):
300g arborio rice, 250g mushrooms, 1 onion, 2 cloves garlic, 150ml white wine, 1L vegetable broth, 50g parmesan, 2 tbsp butter, 1 tbsp olive oil, salt, pepper

Instructions (one step per line):
1. Heat the broth in a pot and keep it warm on low heat.
2. Slice the mushrooms and dice the onion and garlic.
3. Sauté mushrooms in olive oil until golden, then set aside.
4. In the same pan, melt butter and cook onion and garlic until soft.
5. Add rice and stir for 1 minute until lightly toasted.
6. Pour in white wine and stir until absorbed.
7. Add broth one ladle at a time, stirring and waiting for each to absorb.
8. After about 18 minutes, stir in mushrooms and grated parmesan.
9. Season with salt and pepper and serve immediately.

Title:
Chocolate Lava Cake

Description:
A rich individual chocolate cake with a warm, gooey molten center.

Ingredients (comma-separated):
200g dark chocolate, 100g butter, 3 eggs, 3 egg yolks, 80g sugar, 2 tbsp flour, butter and cocoa for ramekins

Instructions (one step per line):
1. Preheat oven to 220°C (430°F).
2. Melt chocolate and butter together in a bowl over simmering water.
3. Whisk eggs, egg yolks, and sugar until thick and pale.
4. Fold the melted chocolate mixture into the egg mixture.
5. Gently fold in the flour.
6. Grease 4 ramekins with butter and dust with cocoa powder.
7. Divide the batter evenly among the ramekins.
8. Bake for 12 minutes until the edges are firm but the center is soft.
9. Let cool for 1 minute, then invert onto plates and serve immediately.
